Welcome!

We are a Pentecostal / Charismatic church located in the Newton Leys (Bletchley) area of Milton Keynes. We publicly launched in January 2015 and were the first church to be planted in Newton Leys. We are a multicultural congregation which, by God’s grace, continues to grow! During our services, we also have an excellent children’s ministry!
