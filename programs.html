PROGRAMS

Sunday services

On Sundays at 10.30am we start with a time of lively worship, which is followed by a Bible-based message in which God’s word is applied to our contemporary lives. After the service, we have a time of fellowship in which refreshments are served.
A warm welcome awaits you!

We meet every Sunday, 10:30am - 12 noon at the following address:

Newton Leys Pavilion
Furzey Way (Off Jersey Drive)
Newton Leys, Bletchley
Milton Keynes
MK3 5SP
Newton Leys Pavilion is a new building and so the address probably won’t come up on Sat Nav. On Jersey Drive, continue past the Asda, past Lakeview Lodge Care Home, and then at the roundabout, take the 2nd exit. Newton Leys Pavilion is on your right.
