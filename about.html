ABOUT US:

Our Story

Pastor Stuart and his wife Andrea moved to Milton Keynes from London in 2014. Before moving to Milton Keynes, they were based at Lynwood Christian Fellowship in Tooting where they both served as ministers. Pastor Stuart also had an itinerant preaching and teaching ministry. After moving to Milton Keynes, God spoke to him about planting a church. For a few weeks, Pastor Stuart and Andrea, with three others (Bev, Joe, and Naomi), met in their home and then publicly launched the church on the 18th January 2015. Joy Community Church was the first church to be planted in Newton Leys, and has continued to grow since its launch.

Our beliefs

As a Pentecostal / Charismatic church, we are committed to following God’s word and the leading of God’s Spirit. We believe:

In the Bible as the word of God

In one God - Father, Son and Holy Spirit (the Trinity)

In the death, burial, and resurrection of Jesus Christ

In salvation by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone

In heaven and hell

Our values

Here are some of our key values as a church community:

We believe God is good and has a good plan for our lives

We share the good news about Jesus Christ with others

We value God’s presence and look forward to encountering Him every time we meet

We honour and love each other

We are intentional about believing God for miracles to happen through and amongst us
